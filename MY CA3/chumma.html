Medicines for Your Dog's Health

At Pawprints, we provide a range of essential medicines to help keep your dog healthy and happy. From preventive care to treatment for illnesses, we have everything your furry friend might need. Below are some categories of medicines and their uses:

1. Flea & Tick Prevention

Flea and tick preventatives are vital to protecting your dog from these common parasites. These treatments help prevent infestations and the diseases they carry.

  • Bravecto – A chewable tablet that provides up to 3 months of protection against fleas and ticks.
  • Frontline Plus – A topical treatment that kills fleas, ticks, and chewing lice on dogs.
  • NexGard – A chewable flea and tick prevention pill that works fast and lasts up to 1 month.

2. Pain Relief & Anti-Inflammatories

Dogs experiencing joint pain or inflammation due to arthritis, surgery, or injury can benefit from pain relief and anti-inflammatory medications. Always consult a vet before administering these medicines.

  • Rimadyl – A non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that helps relieve pain and inflammation from arthritis or surgery.
  • Deracoxib – An NSAID used to treat pain and inflammation in dogs, particularly for post-surgical recovery.
  • Meloxicam – An anti-inflammatory used for the relief of arthritis pain and stiffness.

3. Vitamins & Supplements

Vitamins and dietary supplements support your dog’s overall health, especially in older dogs, puppies, or dogs with special dietary needs.

  • Nutramax Cosequin – Joint supplements that support cartilage and joint function, particularly helpful for older or arthritic dogs.
  • Probiotic Supplements – To maintain gut health and aid in digestion. Great for dogs with sensitive stomachs.
  • Fish Oil Supplements – Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, beneficial for skin health, coat quality, and inflammation management.

4. Dewormers

Dewormers help prevent and treat internal parasites, such as roundworms, tapeworms, and hookworms, which can affect your dog’s health.

  • Panacur – A deworming medicine that treats a broad spectrum of parasites, including roundworms, hookworms, and whipworms.
  • Drontal – A widely used dewormer that is effective against common intestinal worms.
  • Interceptor – Prevents heartworm, along with treating other parasites like hookworms and roundworms.

5. Allergy Medications

Allergies are common in dogs and can cause skin irritation, itching, or digestive problems. These medications help manage symptoms.

  • Benadryl – An antihistamine often used to treat allergic reactions in dogs, including itching and swelling.
  • Atopica – A prescription medication used to treat chronic allergic dermatitis in dogs.
  • Cortisone – A steroid used to control itching and inflammation caused by allergies.

6. Heartworm Prevention

Heartworm is a serious and potentially fatal condition in dogs, but it is preventable with the right medication.

  • Heartgard Plus – A chewable heartworm preventative that also protects against roundworms and hookworms.
  • Interceptor Plus – A chewable tablet that prevents heartworm disease and treats intestinal parasites.
  • Sentinel – Protects against heartworms, fleas, and other parasites, and helps prevent some types of intestinal parasites.

Important Note

Always consult your veterinarian before administering any medication to your dog. Dosage and specific medication recommendations depend on factors such as breed, weight, age, and medical history.
