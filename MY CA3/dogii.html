Popular Dog Breeds

Labrador Retriever

Labradors are friendly, loyal, and outgoing dogs, known for being great family pets.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Hip Dysplasia
  • Elbow Dysplasia
  • Obesity

Treatments/Medications:

  • Pain relief (NSAIDs) for joint pain.
  • Weight management diets and regular exercise to prevent obesity.
  • Joint supplements like glucosamine and chondroitin for joint health.

German Shepherd

German Shepherds are intelligent, loyal, and protective, often used in police and military roles.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Hip and Elbow Dysplasia
  • Degenerative Myelopathy
  • Gastric Dilatation-Volvulus (Bloat)

Treatments/Medications:

  • Surgery for severe hip/elbow dysplasia.
  • Pain management (NSAIDs) for joint pain.
  • Preventative care for bloat, including controlled feeding and exercise.

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are known for their friendly, intelligent, and loving nature, making them excellent family dogs.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Hip Dysplasia
  • Cancer (Hemangiosarcoma, Lymphoma)
  • Skin Allergies

Treatments/Medications:

  • Chemotherapy and surgery for cancer treatment.
  • Joint supplements like glucosamine for hip dysplasia.
  • Antihistamines or allergy-specific medications for skin issues.

Beagle

Beagles are friendly, curious, and great companions, often loved for their affectionate nature.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Hip Dysplasia
  • Epilepsy
  • Obesity

Treatments/Medications:

  • Anticonvulsants for epilepsy (e.g., phenobarbital).
  • Joint supplements and weight management diets.

Bulldog

Bulldogs are courageous and loving dogs, often known for their laid-back and friendly nature.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Brachycephalic Syndrome
  • Hip Dysplasia
  • Skin Infections

Treatments/Medications:

  • Surgery for breathing problems (like widening the nostrils).
  • Antibiotics for skin infections.
  • NSAIDs for joint pain relief.

Poodle

Poodles are intelligent, active, and elegant dogs, known for their hypoallergenic coat.

Read More

Common Health Issues:

  • Hip Dysplasia
  • Addison's Disease
  • Cushing’s Disease

Treatments/Medications:

  • Hormone replacement therapy for Addison's disease.
  • Medications to control Cushing’s disease.
  • Joint supplements for hip dysplasia.
