Common Dog Diseases

Understanding the common diseases that affect dogs is crucial for their health. Some diseases include:

  • Canine Parvovirus
  • Distemper
  • Heartworm
  • Hip Dysplasia

Regular check-ups and vaccinations can help prevent these diseases.
